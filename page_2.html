Healthcare Subpoints

Preventive Care

Preventive healthcare focuses on maintaining wellness and preventing illness through regular check-ups, screenings, and immunizations.

  • Vaccinations
  • Health Screenings
  • Nutrition Counseling

Curative Care

Curative care involves the treatment of diseases and medical conditions to restore health through medications, surgeries, and therapies.

  • Medication Management
  • Surgical Procedures
  • Specialist Consultations

Rehabilitative Care

Rehabilitation helps patients recover from illness, injury, or surgery through physical, occupational, and speech therapy.

  • Physical Therapy
  • Occupational Therapy
  • Speech Therapy

Palliative Care

Palliative care aims to improve the quality of life for patients with serious illnesses by managing pain and providing emotional support.

  • Pain Management
  • Emotional Support
  • Family Counseling
